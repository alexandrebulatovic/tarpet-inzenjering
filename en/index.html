SERVICES AND ACTIVITIES

STAIRS

We offer you the widest range of stairs on the market. We create a product for all occasions. If you need a customized solution we have the knowledge and expertise needed to achieve your goal.

Stair types :

  • Straight stairs
  • L-shaped stairs
  • U-shaped stairs
  • Circular stairs
  • Spiral stairs
Steps can be metal or wooden.

GATES AND FENCES

We offer various types of steel fences for mezzanines, galleries, balconies, terraces, passages, etc. The choice of fence is determined by the environment and application.

HALL

We offer the realization of projects of steel structures, especially steel structures of different dimensions, individually tailored to your needs.

Thanks to innovative solutions and individual approach to each building, we give our clients the opportunity to freely design the architecture and size of the interior hall.
